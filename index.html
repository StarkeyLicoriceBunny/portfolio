Style

My style utilizes a mix of simplicity with deeper meanings and hidden dark depths beneath the adorable surface. My personal style usually consists of adorable animals or creatures doing not-so-adorable things. I like to believe that as long as it's properly conveyed, the true horror of an image can peek through at the viewer. Of course, I'm more than comfortable working with other styles as well if needed.
